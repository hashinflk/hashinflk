Common Questions

Do you handle physical printing and framing?

No, #HASH iNfinite Lk is a digital design studio. We provide high-quality design files for banners, birthday photos, and documents, but we do not handle physical printing or framing.

What file formats will I receive?

You will receive print-ready high-resolution files (PDF, JPEG, or PNG) optimized for the specific dimensions you require for your event.

How long does it take for CVs and documents?

Standard documents and CVs are usually completed within 24–48 hours, depending on the complexity of the layout and the amount of text.

Do you edit and manipulate photos?

Yes! We specialize in photo manipulation and enhancement to make your birthday visuals or other photos look professional and vibrant.
